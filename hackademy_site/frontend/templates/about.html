What is it and why did we create it?

In today’s digital age, we believe that everyone, and not just a lucky few, should be given access to learn the necessary programming skills to become more successful in the future. Hackademy aims to provide an environment to do this all in a way that’s fun, accessible, and affordable.

Through this initiative, students will learn problem-solving and programming skills from professional software engineers, build amazing projects, and together shape the future of our country’s digital economy.
